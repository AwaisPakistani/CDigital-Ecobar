EcoBar Explained

EcoBar is a revolutionary ceramic bar, designed and manufactured in South Korea, to enhance fuel efficiency and reduce emissions in vehicles with internal combustion engines. Compatible with both diesel and petrol engines, it offers a one-time application solution that transforms the way vehicles consume fuel.
